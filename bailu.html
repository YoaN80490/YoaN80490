Bailu

Abondance

Skills

Build

Attaque de base

Diagnostic Kick

Deals Lightning DMG equal to 110% of Bailu's ATK to a single enemy.


Compétences

Singing Among Clouds

Heals a single ally for 12.5% of Bailu's Max HP plus 347. Bailu then heals random allies 2 time(s). After each healing, HP restored from the next healing is reduced by 15%.


Ultime

Felicitous Thunderleap

Heals all allies for 14.4% of Bailu's Max HP plus 401. Bailu applies Invigoration to allies that are not already Invigorated. For those already Invigorated, Bailu extends the duration of their Invigoration by 1 turn. The effect of Invigoration can last for 2 turn(s). This effect cannot stack.


Talent

Gourdful of Elixir

After an ally with Invigoration is hit, restores the ally's HP for 5.8% of Bailu's Max HP plus 160. This effect can trigger 2 time(s). When an ally receives a killing blow, they will not be knocked down. Bailu immediately heals the ally for 19.2% of Bailu's Max HP plus 534 HP. This effect can be triggered 1 time per battle.


Technique

Saunter in the Rain

After Technique is used, at the start of the next battle, all allies are granted Invigoration for 2 turn(s).
