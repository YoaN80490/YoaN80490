Yukong

Harmonie

Skills

Build

Attaque de base

Arrowslinger

Deals 110% of Yukong's ATK as Imaginary DMG to a target enemy.


Compétences

Emboldening Salvo

Obtains 2 stack(s) of "Roaring Bowstrings" (to a maximum of 2 stacks). When "Roaring Bowstrings" is active, the ATK of all allies increases by 88%, and every time an ally's turn (including Yukong's) ends, Yukong loses 1 stack of "Roaring Bowstrings." When it's the turn where Yukong gains "Roaring Bowstrings" by using Skill, "Roaring Bowstrings" will not be removed.


Ultime

Diving Kestrel

If "Roaring Bowstrings" is active on Yukong when her Ultimate is used, additionally increases all allies' CRIT Rate by 29.4% and CRIT DMG by 70.2%. At the same time, deals Imaginary DMG equal to 410.4% of Yukong's ATK to a single enemy.


Talent

Seven Layers, One Arrow

Basic ATK additionally deals Imaginary DMG equal to 88% of Yukong's ATK, and increases the Toughness-Reducing DMG of this attack by 100%. This effect can be triggered again after 1 turn(s).


Technique

Chasing the Wind

After using her Technique, Yukong enters Sprint mode for 20 seconds. In Sprint mode, her Movement SPD increases by 35%, and Yukong gains 2 stack(s) of "Roaring Bowstrings" when she enters battle by attacking enemies.
