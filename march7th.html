March 7th

Preservation

Skills

Build

Attaque de base

Frigid Cold Arrow

Deals Ice DMG equal to 110% of March 7th's ATK to a single enemy.


Compétences

The Power of Cuteness

Provides a single ally with a Shield that can absorb DMG equal to 60.8% of March 7th's DEF plus 846 for 3 turn(s). If the ally's current HP percentage is 30% or higher, greatly increases the chance of enemies attacking that ally.


Ultime

Glacial Cascade

Deals Ice DMG equal to 162% of March 7th's ATK to all enemies. Hit enemies have a 50% base chance to be Frozen for 1 turn(s). While Frozen, enemies cannot take action and will receive Additional Ice DMG equal to 66% of March 7th's ATK at the beginning of each turn.


Talent

Girl Power

After a Shielded ally is attacked by an enemy, March 7th immediately Counters, dealing Ice DMG equal to 110% of her ATK. This effect can be triggered 2 time(s) each turn.


Technique

Freezing Beauty

Immediately attacks the enemy. After entering battle, there is a 100% base chance to Freeze a random enemy for 1 turn(s). While Frozen, the enemy cannot take action and will take Additional Ice DMG equal to 50% of March 7th's ATK at the beginning of each turn.
