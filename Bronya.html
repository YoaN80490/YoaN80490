Bronya

Harmonie

Skills

Build

Attaque de base

Windrider Bullet

Deals Wind DMG equal to 110% of Bronya's ATK to a single enemy.


Compétences

Combat Redeployment

Dispels a debuff from a single ally, allows them to immediately take action, and increases their DMG by 72.6% for 1 turn(s). When this Skill is used on Bronya herself, she cannot immediately take action again.


Ultime

The Belobog March

Increases the ATK of all allies by 59.4%, and increases their CRIT DMG equal to 16.8% of Bronya's CRIT DMG plus 21.6% for 2 turn(s).


Talent

Leading the Way

After using her Basic ATK, Bronya's next action will be Advanced Forward by 33%.


Technique

Banner of Command

After using Bronya's Technique, at the start of the next battle, all allies' ATK increases by 15% for 2 turn(s).
