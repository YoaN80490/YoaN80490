Blade

Skills

Build

Attaque de base

Shard Sword

Deals 110% of Blade's ATK as Wind DMG to a target enemy.


Forest of Swords

Consumes HP equal to 10% of Blade's Max HP and deals Wind DMG equal to the sum of 44% of his ATK and 110% of his Max HP to a single enemy. In addition, deals Wind DMG equal to the sum of 17.6% of Blade's ATK and 44% of his Max HP to adjacent targets. If Blade's current HP is insufficient, his HP will be reduced to 1 when using Forest of Swords. Forest of Swords cannot regenerate Skill Points.


Compétences

Hellscape

Consumes HP equal to 30% of Blade's Max HP to enter the Hellscape state. When Hellscape is active, his Skill cannot be used, his DMG dealt increases by 45.6%, and his Basic ATK Shard Sword is enhanced to Forest of Swords for 3 turn(s). If Blade's current HP is insufficient, his HP will be reduced to 1 when he uses his Skill. This Skill does not regenerate Energy. Using this Skill does not end the current turn.


Ultime

Death Sentence

Sets Blade's current HP to 50% of his Max HP and deals Wind DMG to a single enemy equal to the sum of 43.2% of his ATK, 108% of his Max HP, and 108% of the tally of Blade's HP loss in the current battle. At the same time, deals Wind DMG to adjacent targets equal to the sum of 17.3% of his ATK, 43.2% of his Max HP, and 43% of the tally of his HP loss in the current battle. The tally of Blade's HP loss in the current battle is capped at 90% of his Max HP. This value will be reset and re-accumulated after his Ultimate has been used.


Talent

Shuhu's Gift

When Blade sustains DMG or consumes his HP, he gains 1 stack of Charge, stacking up to 5 times. A max of 1 Charge stack can be gained every time he is attacked. When Charge stack reaches maximum, immediately launches a follow-up attack on all enemies, dealing Wind DMG equal to 48.4% of Blade's ATK plus 121% of his Max HP. At the same time, restores Blade's HP by 25% of his Max HP. After the follow-up attack, all Charges are consumed.


Technique

Karma Wind

Immediately attacks the enemy. After entering combat, consumes 20% of Blade's Max HP while dealing Wind DMG equal to 40% of his Max HP to all enemies. If Blade's current HP is insufficient, his HP will be reduced to 1 when this Technique is used.
